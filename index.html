About Block Management Services

Founded by Louis Armanini, a builder with 30 years in construction — starting in the trades, running field crews, and managing residential and commercial projects from the ground up to the executive suite.

Block Management Services exists because construction's biggest losses rarely come from bad building — they come from slow bids, thin documentation, and paperwork that eats the day. We attack that from both sides: managing projects directly, and building the software and AI workflows that make field teams faster.

When we recommend a tool, it's because we've used it on real construction problems. When we build one, it's because the field needed it and nobody had built it right.
